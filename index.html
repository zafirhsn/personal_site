Hi, I’m Zafir

I am a full-stack web developer based in New York City. I grew up in Queens and continue to live here with my family. I took my first programming classes during my time at Stuyvvesant High School and quickly became interested in the power of code. After Stuyvesant, I attended NYU Tandon, where I graduated with a bachelors degree in computer science. Starting in my senior year, I co-founded IndoorBuddy with 3 other NYU graduates. I am currently still at IndoorBuddy where I work on both our back-end web APIs and front-end user interfaces. I am mainly a JavaScript developer, but have done work in Python and C++ as well. Throughout my time programming, I’ve come to love doing a bit of everything from the back-end to front-end to databases and machine learning. I’ve worked on exciting projects over the years and am excited for what’s next. I like fast-paced environments where I can push myself and learn new things along the way. Most of all, I like solving problems through my code. If you’d like to contact me, you can reach me through this site, LinkedIn, or my email: zh754@nyu.edu
